Jul 3, 2015

5 mins read

Sublime Text 3
Sublime text 3

預設的設定值在Preferences>Settings-Default

[![](http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-BHtrsMvmUSc/VZXzTQv_MKI/AAAAAAAACVo/taqlyOEkpa8/s1600/01.%25E9%25A0%2590%25E8%25A8%25AD%25E8%25A8%25AD%25E5%25AE%259A%25E6%25AA%2594.png)](http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-BHtrsMvmUSc/VZXzTQv_MKI/AAAAAAAACVo/taqlyOEkpa8/s1600/01.%25E9%25A0%2590%25E8%25A8%25AD%25E8%25A8%25AD%25E5%25AE%259A%25E6%25AA%2594.png)

如果要變更設定，需要打開Preferences>Settings-User
[![](http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-6FtW1vtnTW4/VZXzTeGHcgI/AAAAAAAACVg/A4Izoa5_Y5U/s1600/02.%25E5%2594%25AF%25E8%25AE%2580%25E7%259A%2584%25E8%25A8%25AD%25E5%25AE%259A%25E6%25AA%2594.png)](http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-6FtW1vtnTW4/VZXzTeGHcgI/AAAAAAAACVg/A4Izoa5_Y5U/s1600/02.%25E5%2594%25AF%25E8%25AE%2580%25E7%259A%2584%25E8%25A8%25AD%25E5%25AE%259A%25E6%25AA%2594.png)
在這個檔案中把預設的設定值覆蓋即可
